Dineflow

Dineflow is a restaurant discovery platform that helps diners find live offers, events, and menus, while enabling restaurants to increase footfall through real-time updates.

Context

Helping people decide where to dine.

  • Diners often struggle to decide where to eat due to fragmented and outdated restaurant information across platforms
  • Diners cannot easily find real-time offers or events
  • Restaurant menus and deals are often outdated or scattered across platforms
  • Restaurants struggle to promote live offers effectively to nearby users
Target Users
person

Diners

Urban users looking to quickly decide where to eat, especially during peak times. They want easy access to menus, offers, and events, but struggle with outdated, scattered information and lack of real-time visibility.

storefront

Restaurant Owners

Small to mid-sized restaurants aiming to increase footfall and fill tables. They need better ways to promote offers and events, but face limited reach, platform dependency, and high competition.

Problem

Discovery is broken.

Diners — Current Issues

  • Information is often outdated, with offers, menus, and availability not reflecting real-time conditions
  • Users lack visibility into what's happening right now, including live events and active deals
  • Users experience high decision friction by switching between multiple sources to make a choice

Restaurant Owners — Current Issues

  • Restaurants lack the ability to control demand in real time through instant offers or updates
  • Empty tables during off-peak hours result in lost revenue with no effective recovery mechanism
  • Restaurants lack a direct communication channel to reach nearby users at the right moment
Insights

What I realized.

The value isn't in listing restaurants, but in surfacing what matters right now. What users need is decision-ready information at the exact moment of choice. Because dining decisions happen in minutes, not days.
