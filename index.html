My Experience

The Evolution to Product

Technical Support Analyst — SOTI

I have experience working as a Technical Support Analyst, where I developed a strong foundation in problem-solving, customer empathy, and cross-functional collaboration. In this role, I handled user issues end-to-end—diagnosing technical problems, identifying root causes, and ensuring timely resolutions while maintaining a high standard of customer satisfaction. I frequently worked with engineering and product teams to escalate bugs, provide actionable feedback, and improve overall system performance. This exposure gave me a deeper understanding of user pain points and product behavior, which sparked my interest in product management. Over time, I began thinking beyond issue resolution—focusing on why problems occur and how products can be improved to prevent them altogether.

About Me

I'm Nivedh, a Product Manager with a strong user-first mindset, shaped by my experience in technical support. Working closely with users gave me a practical understanding of how people interact with products—their expectations, common challenges, and where experiences can be improved.

Over time, this exposure inspired me to move into a role where I can take a more proactive approach—contributing to product decisions instead of just reacting to issues. That's what drew me to product management: the opportunity to shape better experiences from the start and solve problems more effectively.

I bring a practical, user-driven perspective to product thinking—focusing on identifying gaps, simplifying complexity, and contributing to solutions that deliver real value, regardless of traditional product experience.
