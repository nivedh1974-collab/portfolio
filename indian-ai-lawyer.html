Indian AI Lawyer

An AI system that answers Indian legal questions with citations from real law documents — built for accuracy, not just answers.

Try it on Hugging Face
Context

Helping people understand their rights.

  • Most Indians cannot afford a lawyer for basic legal questions
  • Generic AI gives plausible-sounding but uncited answers — dangerous for legal context
  • Legal documents like BNS 2023 are public but inaccessible to common people
  • No free tool exists that grounds Indian law answers in actual source documents
Target Users
person

Citizens

Urban and rural Indians who need quick, reliable answers to legal questions — about rights, penalties, or procedures — but cannot afford professional legal consultation.

menu_book

Law Students & Researchers

Students and researchers who need to quickly look up sections, cross-reference acts, and verify citations across BNS, BNSS, BSA, and the Constitution.

Problem

Legal AI is broken.

Citizens — Current Issues

  • AI tools hallucinate legal answers with no source citations
  • People cannot verify if an AI answer is actually from law
  • Existing legal apps are expensive or require lawyer subscription
  • New laws like BNS 2023 replaced IPC but most tools still reference outdated acts
Insights

What I realized.

The problem isn't access to AI — it's trust. A legal answer without a citation is just an opinion. What people need is an AI that shows its work: exactly which act, which section, which page.

The Idea

Ground every answer in law.

⚖️

Citation Enforcement

AI explicitly refuses to answer if source documents don't support it. No hallucination.

📄

Real Law Documents

Answers drawn from BNS 2023, BNSS 2023, BSA 2023, Constitution of India, and Kerala Motor Vehicles Act.

🔍

Instant Discovery

Ask in plain English. Get cited answers in seconds. No legal jargon needed.
